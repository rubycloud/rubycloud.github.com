Mission

We seek to build, as Buckminster Fuller put it:

a world that works for 100% of humanity,
in the shortest possible time,
through spontaneous cooperation,
without ecological offense,
or the disadvantage of anyone.

We believe in the capacity of human creativity and collective action to change the course of history.

Founder

Ruby cloud was founded by Matt Olson, a systems thinker, hacker, and social entrepreneur. Matt graduated from u.c. berkeley with degrees in computer science and physics and has been working professionally on web apps for 15 years.

Matt has broad and deep experience in building web and desktop applications, at every level of the process — from initial concept, design and planning, to all aspects of implementation, qa, security audit, operations, performance tuning, and scaling.

For a more detailed description of matt's professional background, check out his résumé.
